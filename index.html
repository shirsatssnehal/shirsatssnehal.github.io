Research & Vision

Bringing research into real-world renewable decisions

My doctoral work strengthens this website beyond a professional profile — it shows how I think about the future of project feasibility, investment screening, and more intelligent development frameworks.

Hybrid Feasibility Decision Model (HFDM)

My PhD research focuses on improving renewable energy project feasibility assessment by integrating practical variables such as CAPEX, OPEX, interconnection, queue position, and financial decision metrics into a more realistic decision model. The goal is to better align academic methods with actual Independent Power Producer development practice.

Focused on utility-scale solar, wind, and battery storage project environments.
Bridges academic analysis with real development and screening realities.
Supports better go / no-go project decisions.
Adds strategic depth beyond standard project management experience.
