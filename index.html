Research

PhD work with practical industry relevance

My doctoral research is focused on making renewable project feasibility assessment more realistic, more data-driven, and more aligned with actual Independent Power Producer decision-making.

Hybrid Feasibility Decision Model (HFDM)

The goal of this work is to bridge the gap between academic feasibility methods and real-world renewable development practice by integrating practical metrics such as CAPEX, OPEX, interconnection, queue position, and financial screening criteria into a stronger decision framework.
